Placement Statistics – Academic Year 2023–2024

Institution: Chennai Institute of Technology

This dataset contains structured placement performance metrics for Chennai Institute of Technology for the academic year 2023–2024.

The data represents overall placement outcomes, salary distribution, student participation, and high-package offer statistics for that academic cycle.

The purpose of this dataset is to provide factual placement performance indicators that can be used to answer queries related to student placements, job offers, salary packages, and career outcomes.

What This Data Represents

The dataset may include:

Overall placement percentage

Total number of offers received

Highest salary package offered (in LPA – Lakhs Per Annum)

Number of offers above certain salary thresholds (e.g., ₹10 LPA, ₹20 LPA)

Average packages for top-performing students

Total eligible students

Students registered for placements

Students placed

Students opting for higher studies
