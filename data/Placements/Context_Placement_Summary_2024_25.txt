Placement Participation Summary – Academic Year 2024–2025
Institution: Chennai Institute of Technology

This dataset contains the placement participation and outcome summary for Chennai Institute of Technology for the academic year 2024–2025.

It provides a structured overview of student strength, placement participation, higher studies distribution, and overall placement success rate for that academic year.

This dataset focuses on student placement participation metrics rather than salary distribution.

What This Data Represents

The dataset includes:

Total Strength – Total number of eligible final-year students.

Registered for Placements – Number of students who participated in campus recruitment.

Opted for Higher Studies – Students who chose postgraduate education instead of placements.

Number of Students Placed – Students who secured at least one job offer.

Placement Percentage – Percentage of placed students calculated based on registered students.
