The following data represents placement statistics of Chennai Institute of Technology for a recent academic year. These metrics describe student placement performance, salary packages offered, and distribution of high-paying job offers.

The values reflect overall placement success rate, total job offers received by students, highest salary package secured, number of premium salary offers, and average packages earned by top-performing students.

Metric Definitions

Overall Placement Percentage refers to the percentage of eligible students who secured job offers.

Total Offers indicates the total number of job offers received across all departments.

Highest Package represents the highest annual salary package offered to a student (measured in LPA – Lakhs Per Annum).

Offers Above ₹20 LPA indicates the number of job offers with salary packages greater than ₹20 Lakhs Per Annum.

Offers Above ₹10 LPA indicates the number of job offers with salary packages greater than ₹10 Lakhs Per Annum.

Top 10% Average Package represents the average salary package earned by the top 10% highest-performing placed students.

Top 20% Average Package represents the average salary package earned by the top 20% highest-performing placed students.
