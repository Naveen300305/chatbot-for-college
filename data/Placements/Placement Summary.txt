Summary of placements for 2022 - 2026 batch:

Chennai Institute of Technology continues to set new benchmarks in career outcomes through its strong and structured placement ecosystem. The 2026 Placement Season reflects the institute’s unwavering focus on academic excellence, industry alignment, and holistic student development. With an impressive 95.8% placement record, our students have secured over 1,400 job offers from leading national and international organizations across diverse sectors. The highest salary package of ₹60 LPA highlights the global  competitiveness and industry relevance of our graduates.

A substantial number of students achieved high-value offers, with 86 students placed in the ₹20 LPA and above category and 174 students securing packages above ₹10 LPA. The performance of the top cohorts remains consistently strong, with the top 10% of students achieving an average package of ₹27.24 LPA and the top 20% averaging ₹18.94 LPA.

These achievements are driven by a comprehensive placement framework that includes:
Industry-integrated and outcome-based curriculum
Continuous skill development
Competency training
Structured aptitude, technical, and soft skills programs
Mentorship by experienced faculty and industry professionals
Strong and sustained partnerships with reputed recruiters
The institute’s Career Development Centre works proactively to bridge the gap between academic learning and industry expectations, ensuring that students are professionally prepared and confident to take on real-world challenges.

Chennai Institute of Technology remains committed to nurturing future-ready engineers and professionals, while strengthening its role as a reliable talent partner for industry and society.
