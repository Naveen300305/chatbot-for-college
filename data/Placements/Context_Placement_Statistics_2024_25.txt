The following data represents the placement summary statistics of Chennai Institute of Technology for the academic year 2024–2025.

This dataset provides a structured breakdown of student participation in placements and overall placement performance for that year.

It includes total student strength, number of students who registered for campus placements, number of students who opted for higher studies, total number of students placed, and the overall placement percentage.

Metric Explanation

Total Strength refers to the total number of final-year eligible students in the 2024–2025 batch.

Registered for Placements indicates the number of students who actively participated in campus recruitment.

Opted for Higher Studies represents students who chose postgraduate education instead of participating in placements.

Number of Students Placed indicates the number of registered students who secured at least one job offer.

Placement % represents the percentage of placed students calculated based on the number of students registered for placements.
