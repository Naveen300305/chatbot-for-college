Industry Trends and Salary Insights - Chennai Institute of Technology
---------------------------------------------------------------------

High Demand Fields in 2025:
1. Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning
   - One of the fastest growing fields globally
   - High demand in IT, healthcare, finance, retail
   - Average fresher salary: Rs. 6-12 LPA

2. Cybersecurity
   - Critical need across all industries
   - Shortage of skilled professionals worldwide
   - Average fresher salary: Rs. 5-10 LPA

3. Cloud Computing
   - AWS, Azure, GCP driving massive hiring
   - Every company moving to cloud
   - Average fresher salary: Rs. 6-10 LPA

4. Data Science and Analytics
   - Data-driven decisions in every industry
   - High demand in BFSI, e-commerce, healthcare
   - Average fresher salary: Rs. 5-10 LPA

5. VLSI and Semiconductor Design
   - India becoming semiconductor hub (CHIPS Act effect)
   - Government incentives attracting global chip makers
   - Average fresher salary: Rs. 6-15 LPA

6. Embedded Systems and IoT
   - Automotive, industrial, smart home sectors growing
   - Electric vehicle revolution driving embedded demand
   - Average fresher salary: Rs. 4-8 LPA

7. Robotics and Automation
   - Manufacturing moving to Industry 4.0
   - High demand in automobile and FMCG sectors
   - Average fresher salary: Rs. 4-8 LPA

8. Renewable Energy / Green Tech
   - Solar and wind energy sector booming in India
   - Government push for 500 GW renewable by 2030
   - Average fresher salary: Rs. 3.5-6 LPA
